Virtual Reality Therapy

This research project is being completed with help of the S.O.U.R.C.E program at Syracuse University


For the 2020-2021 academic year, Rebecca Schmidt, a senior Computer Art and Animaiton major, is devoting her thesis to researching the impact that virtual reality can have on patients as a form of therapy treatment. In addition to research, she is developing her own VR experience based on the research she collects.
