Progress


In May 2020, I began to learn programs that would help me model, texture, animate, sculpt, and develop a virtual reality game. These programs include Photoshop, Blender, Houdini, Substance Painter, Autodesk Maya, Zbrush, and Unreal Engine. In order to develop a realistic environment I would need models of rocks, trees, bushes, plants, flowers, and terrain, as well as materials for all of those assets. By August 2020 I was able to begin developing assets to use in the game. On this page you will see the most recent verisons of models, terrains, and some materials, as the project continues to be developed.
